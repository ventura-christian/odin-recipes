Tofu with Rice and Veggies

By Christian Ventura

Updated: February 22, 2025

Description

This tofu with rice and veggies is a healthy, flavorful, and satisfying dish. The tofu is pan-fried until crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, while the veggies are stir-fried with garlic, soy sauce, and a touch of sweetness. Served with rice, it’s a perfect meal that’s both nutritious and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu
  • 1 cup rice (white or brown)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (or your preferred oil)
  • 1 medium carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup or honey (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish, optional)
  • Green onions (for garnish, optional)

Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Rice

Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
Set aside.
Step 2: Press the Tofu

Press the tofu to remove excess moisture.
You can wrap it in a clean towel and place something heavy on top for about 10 minutes.
Then, cut the tofu into cubes.
Step 3: Pan-fry the Tofu

Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Add the tofu cubes and cook until golden and crispy on all sides, about 6-8 minutes.
Remove from the skillet and set aside.
Step 4: Stir-fry the Veggies

In the same skillet, add the remaining olive oil.
Add the onion and garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
Add the carrot, bell pepper, and zucchini. Stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until the veggies are tender but still crisp.
Step 5: Add Flavor

Return the tofu to the skillet.
Add the soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and maple syrup (if using).
Stir to coat everything evenly and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
Step 6: Serve and Enjoy!

Serve the tofu and veggie mixture over the cooked rice.
Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions if desired.
Dig in and enjoy your delicious tofu with rice and veggies!
