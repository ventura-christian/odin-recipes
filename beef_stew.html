Beef Stew with Carrots and Potatoes

By Christian Ventura

Updated: February 19, 2025

Description

This classic beef stew recipe is sure to entice with its rich aromas, hearty broth, and mouth watering ingredients. Slow cooked in a dutch oven, the meat will fall off the bones and is guaranteed to warm you up during the cold winter months.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs beef (chuck or stew meat), cut into chunks
  • 2 tbsp oil (vegetable or olive)
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp flour (optional, for thickening)

Steps

Step 1: Sear the Beef

Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
Season the beef with salt and pepper, sear until browned on all sides.
Remove and set aside.
Step 2: Saut√© the Vegetables

In the same pot, add onions, garlic, and carrots.
Cook for a few minutes until softened.
Step 3: Add Liquids and Seasoning

Stir in tomato paste and Worcestershire sauce.
Pour in beef broth, add thyme and bay leaf.
Step 4: Simmer the Stew

Return the beef to the pot.
Cover and simmer on low heat for about 1.5 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
Step 5: Add Potatoes and Final Adjust

Add diced potatoes and cook for another 30 minutes until tender.
If you want a thicker stew, mix 1 tbsp flour with water and stir it in.
Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

Remove bay leaf, taste, and adjust seasoning if needed.
Serve warm and enjoy!
